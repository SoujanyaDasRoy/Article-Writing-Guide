# Prompt 6 (For Quality Checking)

## 1. Source Verification Check

### Must Pass

* All factual claims have official sources
* Dates are verified from official notifications
* Eligibility criteria match official guidelines
* Financial figures are officially confirmed
* Authority names/designations are correct
* Circular/notification references are mentioned
* Source publication dates are included
* Statistics include retrieval/source context

### Fail Conditions

* No official references
* Unverified statistics
* Assumed dates or deadlines
* Third-party blogs used as primary source

---

## 2. Timeline Accuracy Check

### Must Pass

* All dates clearly labeled as:

  * Confirmed
  * Tentative
  * Historical
  * Expected
* Session/year validity is mentioned
* “Last Updated” date is visible
* Expired deadlines are removed or marked

### Fail Conditions

* Ambiguous deadlines
* Mixed historical/current timelines
* Old notifications presented as active

---

## 3. Editorial Tone Check

### Must Pass

* Neutral and professional language
* No promotional phrasing
* No exaggerated claims
* No emotional manipulation
* No conversational filler

### Avoid Phrases Like

* “Definitely apply”
* “Amazing opportunity”
* “Don’t miss this”
* “Hurry up”
* “Best scholarship ever”

### Preferred Tone

* Informational
* Objective
* Policy-oriented
* Reader-focused

---

## 4. Structure & Hierarchy Check

### Must Pass

* Proper heading hierarchy:

  * H1
  * H2
  * H3
  * H4
* Logical section flow
* Clear subsection separation
* Consistent formatting

### Recommended Structure

* Introduction
* Overview
* Eligibility
* Benefits
* Dates
* Documents
* Application Process
* Important Notes
* Safety Advisory
* Official Sources
* Disclaimer

### Fail Conditions

* Random heading order
* Missing subsections
* Dense text walls
* Poor readability

---

## 5. Data Formatting Check

### Must Pass

* Tables properly aligned
* Mobile-readable formatting
* Consistent date formatting
* Clear labels and headers
* Bullet lists used where necessary

### Fail Conditions

* Broken tables
* Misaligned columns
* Large unformatted paragraphs
* Inconsistent formatting styles

---

## 6. SEO Quality Check

### Must Pass

* Natural keyword usage
* Semantic keyword variations
* Readable metadata/title
* Human-first writing
* Proper internal structure

### Fail Conditions

* Keyword stuffing
* Repetitive phrasing
* AI-style over-optimization
* Forced keyword placement

---

## 7. AI-Generated Content Detection Check

### Must Pass

* Concise writing
* Natural sentence variation
* No repetitive filler
* Human editorial refinement
* Contextual depth present

### Common AI Signals

* Repeated sentence structures
* Generic explanations
* Excessive transitions
* Empty informational padding

### Fail Conditions

* Robotic tone
* Redundant paragraphs
* Surface-level writing

---

## 8. Context & Depth Check

### Must Pass

* Explains policy relevance
* Includes educational significance
* Adds societal/student impact
* Gives contextual background
* Explains why the scheme matters

### Fail Conditions

* Purely procedural content
* No contextual reporting
* No relevance framing

---

## 9. User Safety & Fraud Prevention Check

### Must Pass

* Official portal warning included
* Scam/phishing warning present
* OTP/password advisory included
* Fraud reporting guidance added

### Mandatory Advisory Topics

* Use only official websites
* Never share OTPs/passwords
* Avoid unofficial agents
* Verify payment links/messages

### Fail Conditions

* No safety guidance
* External unknown links
* Unsafe recommendations

---

## 10. Disclaimer & Verification Check

### Must Pass

* Rules may change disclaimer
* Official notifications override article info
* Readers advised to verify updates
* Portal verification recommendation included

### Fail Conditions

* No disclaimer section
* Article presents temporary info as permanent

---

## 11. Readability & UX Check

### Must Pass

* Easy scanning
* Short paragraphs
* Proper spacing
* Important info highlighted
* Mobile readability maintained

### Fail Conditions

* Long dense blocks
* Poor spacing
* Confusing navigation

---

## 12. Grammar & Language Check

### Must Pass

* Grammar reviewed
* Spelling verified
* Consistent tense usage
* Professional punctuation
* No broken sentences

### Fail Conditions

* Typographical errors
* Grammar inconsistencies
* Informal/slang language

---

## 13. Compliance & Legal Risk Check

### Must Pass

* No misleading claims
* No unofficial guarantees
* No fake authority implication
* Financial information verified
* Official scheme naming accurate

### Fail Conditions

* Misrepresentation
* Incorrect government references
* Unsupported claims

---

## 14. Statistics & Numbers Validation Check

### Must Pass

Every statistic has:

* Source
* Retrieval date
* Reporting period

Numbers are contextualized.

Live data is clearly identified.

### Fail Conditions

* Unsupported figures
* Outdated data
* Contextless statistics

---

## 15. Introduction Quality Check

### Must Pass

* Professional opening
* Clear overview
* Policy context included
* Immediate informational clarity

### Fail Conditions

* Blog-style opening
* Casual tone
* Generic filler introduction

---

## 16. Conclusion Quality Check

### Must Pass

* Neutral summary
* Verification reminder
* Clear next-step guidance
* Official source recommendation

### Fail Conditions

* Promotional ending
* Emotional CTA language
* No verification reminder

---

## 17. Accessibility & Inclusivity Check

### Must Pass

* Simple understandable language

* Clear explanation of terms

### Fail Conditions
